# Privacy Policy

**Last updated:** 21 July 2026

CallPilot ("CallPilot", "we", "us") provides an AI voice-agent service that gathers price quotes and negotiates on your behalf. This policy explains what we collect, why, who we share it with, and the choices you have.

It also covers something most policies don't need to: **information about the businesses we contact on your behalf**, who are affected by this service even though they are not our users.

---

## 1. Information we collect

### Account information
Your email address and authentication credentials, handled by Supabase Auth. If you sign in with Google, we receive only the basic profile information Google shares — your name, email address, and profile picture. We never receive your Google password.

### Job details
The information you give us about the job you want priced. For a move, that means addresses, dates, property size, access constraints such as stairs or lifts, large items, and any notes you add. You provide this in one of three ways:

- **A voice interview** with our AI interviewer, which is recorded and transcribed.
- **An uploaded photo or document** — for example an existing quote or an inventory list — which is read by an AI vision model to extract structured details.
- **The form**, typed by you.

You review and confirm these details before anything is used. Once confirmed, the same specification is reused word-for-word on every call, which is what makes the quotes comparable.

### Call recordings and transcripts
To gather and negotiate quotes, CallPilot holds voice conversations and **records and transcribes them**. This applies both to your voice interview and to conversations with businesses.

- Our agent **states at the start of every call that it is an AI calling on behalf of a customer**, and says so plainly if asked.
- Recordings and transcripts are stored against your account so your report can cite the exact evidence behind each quote and each negotiated change.
- In **simulation mode**, conversations are between two AI agents and no external party is contacted. In **live telephony mode**, real businesses are called.

The businesses we call are not our users and have not agreed to our terms. We limit what we collect from them to what the conversation itself contains: what was said, the quoted prices and conditions, and the business's publicly listed contact details.

### Usage information
Standard technical data — IP address, browser type, timestamps — plus how many comparisons your account has remaining, used to run the service, keep it secure, and enforce usage limits.

### Payment information
CallPilot does not currently take payments and we do not collect or store card details.

---

## 2. How we use it

- To build your job specification and confirm it with you before any call is placed
- To place calls, gather quotes, and negotiate on your behalf
- To produce your ranked comparison report, including the transcript evidence behind it
- To run your account, apply usage limits, and keep the service secure
- To investigate failures and improve reliability
- To meet legal obligations

**We do not sell your personal information**, and we do not use your call recordings or transcripts to train AI models.

---

## 3. Who we share it with

We share data with the following providers, only so they can perform their part of the service:

| Provider | What it receives | Why |
|---|---|---|
| Supabase | Account details, job data, quotes | Authentication and database storage |
| ElevenLabs | Voice audio, call transcripts | Voice AI and conversation handling |
| OpenAI | Uploaded images, job details, transcript text | Document extraction and report generation |
| Tavily | Business category and location | Finding businesses to contact |
| Twilio | Phone numbers, call audio | Placing outbound calls in live telephony mode |
| Render, Vercel | Application traffic | Hosting |

We may also disclose information where legally required, or to protect the rights, property, or safety of CallPilot, our users, or others.

---

## 4. How long we keep it

Job details, recordings, transcripts, and reports are kept while your account is active, so you can revisit past comparisons. Delete a job and its recordings and transcripts are removed. Delete your account and we remove your personal data within 30 days, except where we are required to retain something by law.

You can ask us to delete any of this sooner at any time.

---

## 5. Your rights

Depending on where you live, you may have the right to access the personal information we hold about you, correct it, delete it, export it, withdraw consent where processing relies on consent, and object to certain uses.

To exercise any of these, email **spoodermansee@gmail.com** and we will respond within 30 days.

## 6. Security

Data is encrypted in transit using TLS and encrypted at rest by our database provider. Access to your data is enforced at the database level, so one account cannot read another's records. Authentication is handled by Supabase using signed, expiring tokens.

No system is perfectly secure, and we do not claim otherwise.
